# Capitalization Size
<ol> 
<li> Micro Cap (Under $300M) riskiest</li>

<li>Mid Cap ($3B to $15B) balanced</li>
<li>Large Cap ($15B to $100B) safe</li>
<li>Mega Cap (Over $100B) super safe</li>
</ol>
